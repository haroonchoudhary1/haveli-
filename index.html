About Us

Located NEAR S.K BRIDGE, CHRIST SCHOOL ROAD, POONCH 185101, Haveli Restaurant brings you the flavors of Kashmir with a modern twist. From traditional recipes to new-age presentation, every dish is prepared with love and fresh ingredients. Our ambiance is a blend of heritage charm and modern comfort, making it perfect for families, friends, and foodies alike.
